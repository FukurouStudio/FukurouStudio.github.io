SnapMeta Privacy Policy

Developer: Fukurou Studio

SnapMeta does not collect, store, or share personal data directly.

The app reads EXIF metadata only from photos explicitly selected by the user. All processing is performed locally on the user's device. No photos or metadata are uploaded to external servers.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect and process information such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, and usage data for advertising and analytics purposes.
